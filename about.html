Started with one overwhelmed founder.

Our first client came to us juggling a Shopify developer, a separate SEO freelancer, and an agency for social — none of whom talked to each other. The store looked disjointed because it was built that way.

We took over all three, put them under one roadmap, and the store's conversion rate doubled in ten weeks. That became the model: one team, every channel, one plan.

Today we run the same playbook for Walmart, Facebook Marketplace, and TikTok Shop sellers who'd rather focus on sourcing and product than platform mechanics.
